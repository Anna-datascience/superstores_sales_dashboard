📌 Project Overview

This dashboard provides a comprehensive view of Superstore business performance across key metrics, enabling data-driven decision-making for sales managers and executives.

Key questions answered:

Which regions and states are most profitable?
How do sales and profit trend over time?
Which product categories and sub-categories drive revenue?
What is the performance across customer segments?
What are the top selling products?

📊 Dashboard Features

Feature  -             Description
KPI Cards    -      Total Sales, Profit, Quantity, Discount
Sales        -      TrendYear-over-year and monthly sales area chart
Regional Map   -     State-level sales density mapping
Category Breakdown - Sales & profit by category and sub-category
Segment Analysis   -  Consumer, Corporate, Home Office performance
Filters          -   Year, Region, Category, Segment — all interactive


🗂️ Dataset

Source: Tableau Sample Superstore
File: Sample - Superstore.csv
Records: ~9,994 orders
Fields include: Order Date, Ship Date, Customer Name, Segment, Region, State, Category, Sub-Category, Sales, Quantity, Discount, Profit
